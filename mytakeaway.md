My overall takeaway from this research

The science is clear: your microbiome is unique to you, it changes in response to what you eat, 
where you live, and what drugs you take, and it has measurable effects on physical and mental health.
Current interventions mostly ignore this individual variation. 
The opportunity is to build a system that actually accounts for it — using: 
genomic sequencing, microbiome analysis, and AI to generate truly personalised interventions. 
That's what my platform concept attempts to do.

The field is moving from FMT (blunt instrument) → defined consortia → engineered probiotics → AI-driven precision formulations. 
My idea sits at that frontier.
